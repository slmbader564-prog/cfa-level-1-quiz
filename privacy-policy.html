Privacy Policy

App: CFA Level 1 Quiz  ·  Version 1.3  ·  Last updated: 20 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the CFA Level 1 Quiz mobile app (the “App”) handles information. The short version: the App does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information. Your study data stays on your device.

Information we collect

None that is sent to us. We do not have user accounts, servers, or analytics. We never ask for your name, email, location, contacts, or any personal identifiers.

Data stored on your device

To make the App work, the following is saved locally on your device only (in the app’s local storage) and is never transmitted to us or anyone else:

  • Your quiz history and scores;
  • In-progress (paused) quizzes;
  • Questions you have flagged or bookmarked;
  • Your spaced-repetition review schedule and per-question study record (used to resurface questions and build your mastery map);
  • Your settings (e.g. number of questions, feedback mode, light/dark theme);
  • Whether you have unlocked the paid “Pro” content.

This data remains on your device. Uninstalling the App, or clearing the App’s storage in your device settings, permanently deletes it.

Purchases

The App offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock all modules. Payments are processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We never see or store your payment-card details. The App only receives confirmation of whether a purchase or restore succeeded, so it can unlock content. Google’s handling of your purchase is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

Third-party services

  • Google Play Billing — processes the in-app purchase (see above).
  • Google Fonts — the App loads display fonts from Google’s font servers. As with any request to a web server, Google may receive your device’s IP address and standard request information solely to deliver the fonts. The App sends no personal data in these requests.

The App contains no advertising, no third-party analytics, and no tracking SDKs.

Children’s privacy

The App is an exam-preparation study aid intended for a general audience (adults preparing for a professional exam). It is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Data security

Because your data never leaves your device, it is protected by your device’s own security. We hold no copy of it.

Your choices

You can delete all App data at any time by clearing the App’s storage or uninstalling the App. There is no account to close because no account exists.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above and post the revised policy at this URL.
